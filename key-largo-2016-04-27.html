This trip is designed for divers completing their Advanced Open Water, Enriched Air (Nitrox), Peak Performance Buoyancy, Deep Diver, and Navigation specialty courses. To participate in this trip you must be an Advanced Student or hold an Advanced Open Water rating and Enriched Air (Nitrox) certifcation.

Remember that you must have Divers Alert Network (DAN) Dive Accident Insurance. I also highly recommend dive travel insurance, which can be purchased from DAN or Dive Assure.

Diving Key Largo

Key Largo is farther from Atlanta than West Palm Beach and more expensive because all the diving is boat-based and the hotels are more expensive. But Key Largo reefs are beautiful and the variety of mostly current-free sites offer the advantage of easily accomodating both open water students and advanced continuing education divers. On one trip we can visit 100 ft deep wrecks like The Duane and The Spiegel Grove and 30 ft deep coral reefs. Key Largo is a dive wonderland!Rainbow Reef.
