Key Largo Training

15-18 February 2016, Printer-ready Detailed Agenda

This trip is designed for students completing their Open Water certification and divers completing Deep and Underwater Navigation specialties. Be sure to select the right option when you sign up!

Remember that you must have Divers Alert Network (DAN) Dive Accident Insurance. I also highly recommend dive travel insurance, which can be purchased from DAN or Dive Assure.

Sign-up: Coming soon!

Includes:

  • 6 dives (see Itinerary for details)
  • 2 or 3 nights at the hotel
  • Tanks and weights
  • All scuba equipment for ProST students
  • If you aren't a ProST or GT student you can rent gear from The Dive Shop, dive operator, or weekend-long full package from ProST for $75
