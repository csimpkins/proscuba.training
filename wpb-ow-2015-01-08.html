West Palm Beach Open Water Cert Trip

8 - 11 January 2015

I'm rescheduling this trip (probably for February) and adding an advanced trip for 16-19 January (MLK weekend). Check back soon for details!

Important Notes

  • Georgia Tech Scuba Scuba 1 students completing their Open Water certifications may rent gear from Georgia Tech free of charge. Other students and certified divers must provide their own gear, or rent gear.
  • If you completed your open water confined water sessions more than one month prior to this trip, please contact me to arrange a skills review pool session.
  • If you completed your training with another instructor outside of GT Scuba, please contact me to arrange a skills review pool session.
  • All students must provide me with their referral form signed by their instructor and an up to date PADI medical statement.
  • All certified divers must provide me with a copy of their certification card.
  • All divers (students and non-students) must provide me with a copy of their DAN cards.

Agenda

Diving at the Blue Heron Bridge is highly dependent on tides and boat charters typically leave on time, so it's very importatnt to plan ahead and stay on schedule. Each West Palm Beach trip is planned for the specific tide predictions and boat schedules in effect for that particular weekend.

Thursday, 8 January 2015
  • Arrive and check-in at Seaspray Inn
Friday, 9 January 2015
  • Students:
    • 09:00 - Check in at Pura Vida Divers, pick up tanks
    • 09:25 - Depart Pura Vida Divers for Blue Heron Bridge
    • 09:30 - Arrive at Blue Heron Bridge
    • 09:40 - Blue Heron Bridge Orientation
    • 09:50 - Set up scuba units, don wet suits
    • 10:00 - Enter water for OW dives 1 and 2 (high tide at 10:44am)
    • 11:15 - Exit water
    • 11:20 - Pack gear, depart for Pura Vida Divers
    • 11:30 - Arrive at Pura Vida divers, return tanks
    • 11:45 - Debrief dive
  • Certified divers:
    • 09:30 - Arrive at Lake Park Marina and check in at boat
    • 10:00 - Boat departs for experience dives 2 and 3
    • 14:00 - Boat arrives back at Lake Park Marina
    • 14:10 - Depart boat, pack cars, depart for Pura Vida Divers
  • Remainder of day free for beach, surfing, golf, etc.
