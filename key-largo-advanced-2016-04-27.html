Key Largo Advanced Trip

27 - 29 April 2016, Printer-ready Detailed Agenda

This trip is designed for divers completing their Advanced Open Water, Enriched Air (Nitrox), Peak Performance Buoyancy, Deep Diver, and Navigation specialty courses. To participate in this trip you must be an Advanced Student or hold an Advanced Open Water rating and Enriched Air (Nitrox) certifcation.

Remember that you must have Divers Alert Network (DAN) Dive Accident Insurance. I also highly recommend dive travel insurance, which can be purchased from DAN or Dive Assure.

Sign-up: Closed

Includes:

  • 8 dives (see Itinerary for details)
  • 2 nights at the Marriott Courtyard Key Largo
  • Tanks and weights
  • All scuba equipment for ProST and GT Scuba students
  • Non ProST or GT Scuba students may rent a full package of top of the line gear from ProST for $75

Getting to Key Largo

  • The closest airport is Palm Beach International (PBI). Fort Lauderdale (FLL) is about an hour's drive. Delta has direct flights to both airports.
  • Driving takes about ten and a half hours from Atlanta to the Marriott Courtyard Key Largo.
