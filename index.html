I'm Chris Simpkins, a PADI Master Scuba Diver Trainer and DAN Instructor in Atlanta, GA. I teach for Georgia Tech Scuba. Pro Scuba Training is my personal business through which I lead dive trips and provide private instruction for individuals and small groups.

WARNING! Once the ocean casts its spell on you you'll never be the same. When you're stuck on land you'll yearn for the underwater world, dream of the ocean, and count the days until your next dive trip. And if your significant other doesn't share your passion for diving, you may want to warn them. :-)
